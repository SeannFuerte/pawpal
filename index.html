Frequently Asked Questions

Here's th FAQ's Section for our dear clients

Booking an appointment with PawPal - Marinduque ProVet Booking is easy! Simply visit our website or mobile app, select your desired service, choose a convenient date and time, and fill out the required information. Alternatively, you can also contact our office directly to schedule an appointment over the phone.
If your pet experiences an emergency outside of our regular operating hours, please contact our emergency hotline immediately. Our experienced veterinarians are available 24/7 to provide urgent medical care to your pet. Remember, prompt action in an emergency can make all the difference in your pet's health and well-being.
While we cannot guarantee the availability of a specific veterinarian for every appointment, we will do our best to accommodate your request whenever possible. All of our veterinarians are highly skilled and experienced, ensuring your pet receives the best possible care regardless of the attending veterinarian.
To ensure a smooth and stress-free veterinary visit, we recommend the following preparations:
-Bring any relevant medical records or vaccination history for your pet.
-Transport your pet in a secure carrier or leash to ensure their safety.
-If your pet is anxious or aggressive, consider bringing them in a muzzle or calming aids recommended by your veterinarian.
-Follow any pre-appointment instructions provided by our team, such as fasting requirements for certain procedures.
